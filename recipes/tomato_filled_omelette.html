Tomato filled omelette


description

I like to eat this kind of omelette.

It's unusual and spicey.


Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • spicey green pepper
  • tomatoes


Steps

  1. Add tomatoes to pan
  2. Add salt and chopped green pepper
  3. Wait until tomatoes and pepper cooked nicely
  4. Remove tomatoes and pepper and put in a plate
  5. Scramble eggs in a bowl
  6. Add eggs to pan
  7. Wait until cooked nicely
  8. Add tomatoes and pepper and cover with one of the omelette halves
  9. eat!
