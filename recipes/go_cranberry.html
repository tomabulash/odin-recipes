Go Cranberry

description

I used to eat a lot of Go dairy free yogurt, it's easy in the morning and doesn't require preperation.

one day I decided to add dried cranberries and the result was so much more satisfying


Ingredients

  • Go dairy-free yogurt (any taste)
  • Dried cranberries


Steps

  1. Open Go dairy-free yogurt
  2. Pour in some yummy dried cranberries
  3. Mix the dried cranberries in, and eat.
